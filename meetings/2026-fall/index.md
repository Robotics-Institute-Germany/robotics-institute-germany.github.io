# RIG General Meeting Fall 2026 in Berlin

## General Information

**Meeting details**
* Begin: September 17, 13:00 
* End: September 18, 14:00 

**Meeting Locations**
- [TU Berlin, Marchstraße 23, 10587](https://maps.app.goo.gl/yZszp4wgrEncJMrD8)
- [Fraunhofer HHI at Lanolin Fabrik](https://maps.app.goo.gl/gMJNbfFDTop1WD1i6)

These are a 6 minute walk away from each other on opposite sides of the Spree (the river).

**Workshops**

If you are interested in hosting a workshop, please fill out the [workshop registration form](https://forms.gle/gLC3rBrhw9gz9evWA).

**Parking**  
While there is free parking around the Marchstr. 23 building on the TU Berlin campus, the chances of finding a parking spot are very low after 9:00.
